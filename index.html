Greg's Instant Coffee Review

The need to find the best instant coffee came from my endeavors into hiking and canoeing in the boundary waters of northern Minnesota. Regardless of location my coffee consumption has become ingrained as a consistent behavior and requirement of my day. It's become a requirement, and not a luxury. Why instant? While there are other methods to get a cup of coffee in the woods none are as weight conscious as instant. While hiking you feel every extra ounce on your back as you pursue your journey. This leaves us with a smaller subset of mostly freeze dried or spray dried coffees. The quest to find the best one has begun. Bear in mind this is all subjective to my personal preferences. As you can see below the weight consideration of different setups has an impact. What do you want to carry? If you're like me it's the ultraweight loadout with instant coffee.

Ultralight - 8 oz
Aeropress - 16 oz
Minipresso - 16 oz
Percolator - 27 oz

My standardized testing method

  1. 212 degree (boiling) water
  2. 8 oz sample size
  3. Titanium Mug
  4. Outdoors
